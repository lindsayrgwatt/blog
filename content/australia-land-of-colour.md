One of the things that has surprised me about Australia is how colourful it is (the land, not the people - I expected that).  In particular, the flowers are incredible.  I have yet to see a [Golden Wattle](http://goaustralia.about.com/od/discoveraustralia/a/goldenwattle.htm) (national flower) or a [Waratah](http://goaustralia.about.com/od/nsw/a/waratah.htm) (New South Wales' state flower), but I've definitely seen a lot of [Hibiscus](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hibiscus).  Here's a sampling of the colours so far:
