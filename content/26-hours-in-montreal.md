Over the course of the weekend I did a whirlwind trip to Montreal with my buddy Rich as he'd never been. 14 hours of driving for 26 hours in the city. It was a gluttonous affair: poutine, ridiculous meat at [Au Pied de Cochon](http://www.restaurantaupieddecochon.ca/), smoked meat at [Schwartz's](http://www.schwartzsdeli.com/index2.html) and too much coffee.
